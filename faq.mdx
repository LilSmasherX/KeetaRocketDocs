## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Keeta Rocket?">
    Keeta Rocket is your comprehensive launchpad into the Keeta ecosystem. It provides all the tools, resources, and guidance you need to get started with Keeta quickly and effectively.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which wallet do we need to participate in token launches?">
    You will need the Lightbeam wallet which can be found here: https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/lightbeam-wallet/ijijinoemjchfganmfclkokjmdgihmjn 

    The Lightbeam wallet is open source, and can be audited here: https://github.com/Deadrare/Lightbeam-Wallet

    Other wallets will also be compatible in the future.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What currency will the token sales be in?">
    You will need KTA on Keeta mainnet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I buy tokens?">
    1. Navigate to https://www.keetarocket.com/
    2. Connect your Lightbeam wallet. (Download the chrome extension here: https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/lightbeam-wallet/ijijinoemjchfganmfclkokjmdgihmjn )
    3. Enter the amount you wish to buy
    4. Click 'Submit'
    5. Sign the transaction in your wallet
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
