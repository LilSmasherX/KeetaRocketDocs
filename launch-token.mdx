## Requirements

Before you launch your project with us, we will need the following information: 

- **Project Details**: Project name, description, website and other links
- **Testnet**: Is your project deployed on Keeta testnet or mainnet? If not, when do you expect it to be? We require a working product on testnet or mainnet before launching. 
- **Key Dates**: Token launch date, mainnet launch date, and any other important milestones
- **Token Details**: Token name, ticker, price, total supply, min and max raise amounts

### Step 3: Review Process

Our team will review your application within 1 week. We may reach out for additional information or clarification during this time.

### Step 4: Announcement

Once approved, we will work with you to announce your token launch date and details to the Keeta Rocket community. This includes:

- Social media announcement
- Adding your project to our launch calendar

### Step 5: Go Live

Your token launch will be featured on the Keeta Rocket platform where community members can:

- View project details and documentation
- Connect their Lightbeam wallets
- Purchase tokens using KTA

<Warning>
Token launches are subject to review and approval. Not all applications will be accepted. Ensure your project meets our quality standards.
</Warning>
